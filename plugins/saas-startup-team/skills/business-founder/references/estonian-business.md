# Estonian Business Environment

## Company Formation: OÜ (Osaühing)

### What is an OÜ?
- Estonian private limited company (equivalent to LLC/GmbH)
- Standard form for startups and small businesses
- Minimum share capital: 0.01 EUR (no minimum capital requirement since 2023)
- Can be founded online via e-Business Register (Äriregister)

### Registration Process
1. **e-Business Register**: https://ariregister.rik.ee/
2. Choose company name (check availability in register)
3. Define articles of association
4. Register online with ID card or e-Residency digital ID
5. Timeline: 1-3 business days for registration
6. Cost: State fee ~265 EUR

### e-Residency
- Digital identity for non-residents to manage Estonian companies
- Enables: company registration, banking, tax filing, document signing
- Application: https://e-resident.gov.ee/
- Cost: 100-120 EUR, processing time: 3-8 weeks

## Tax Environment

### Corporate Tax
- **0% on retained earnings** — only taxed when profits are distributed
- 20% on distributed profits (14% for regular distributions)
- This means reinvested profits are tax-free — ideal for growing SaaS

### VAT (Käibemaks)
- Standard rate: 22% (increased from 20% in 2024)
- Registration threshold: 40,000 EUR annual turnover
- Digital services to EU consumers: VAT at customer's country rate (OSS scheme)
- B2B cross-border EU: reverse charge mechanism

### Employment Tax
- Social tax: 33% (employer's cost on top of gross salary)
- Income tax: 20% flat rate
- Unemployment insurance: 0.8% (employee) + 0.8% (employer)

## Legal Requirements for SaaS

### GDPR Compliance
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) required
- Privacy Policy mandatory
- Cookie consent required
- Data breach notification: 72 hours to supervisory authority
- Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (Andmekaitse Inspektsioon): https://www.aki.ee/

### Terms of Service
- Required for any commercial SaaS product
- Must be in Estonian if targeting Estonian market
- English version acceptable for international products

### Consumer Protection
- 14-day withdrawal right for consumer contracts (EU directive)
- Clear pricing and automatic renewal disclosure
- Complaint handling procedure required

## Banking

### Business Banking Options
- **LHV Pank**: Most startup-friendly Estonian bank
- **Wise Business**: Good for international transactions
- **Swedbank**: Largest Estonian bank, more traditional
- Opening a business account typically requires: company registration, articles of association, proof of business activity

### Payment Processing
- Stripe: Available in Estonia, supports EUR
- Paddle: Good for SaaS (handles VAT compliance)
- Montonio: Estonian payment provider (local banks integration)
